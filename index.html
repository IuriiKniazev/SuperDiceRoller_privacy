Privacy Policy

App Name: Super Dice Roller

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Super Dice Roller ("we", "our", or "the application") handles user information.

1. Personal Data

We do not directly collect personal data such as names, emails, or phone numbers. However, certain data may be automatically processed by third-party services integrated into the application.

2. Analytics (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase), provided by Google LLC, to collect anonymized usage data.

Data collected may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Country-level location
  • Usage events and session statistics
  • Advertising identifiers

This information is used to improve app performance and user experience.

3. Advertising (Yandex Advertising Network)

The application displays advertisements provided by the Yandex Advertising Network.

Advertising partners may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • Ad interaction data

This data may be used to personalize ads and measure their effectiveness.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR – EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the legal basis for processing data is:

  • Your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) – for personalized ads and analytics
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) – to improve app stability and functionality

5. User Rights Under GDPR

If you are an EU user, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

You may exercise these rights by contacting us via email.

6. International Data Transfers

Third-party services such as Google and Yandex may process data outside your country of residence, including outside the European Economic Area. Such transfers are performed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

7. Data Retention

We do not store personal data ourselves. Data retention is determined by the respective third-party service providers.

8. Children’s Privacy

The application is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no digital transmission or storage system is completely secure.
